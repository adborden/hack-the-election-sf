Who is the money coming from?

Each bubble represents a contribution. Its size represents the amount of the contibution. The color of the bubble indicates whether the contribution was made by an individual or some type of organization. Mouse over the bubbles to see who made the contribution and how much they contributed.

Contribution by size

The bubbles are sorted into two groups - Less than 500 and Greater than 500.

Contribution by type

The bubbles are sorted by the type of entity that made the contribution.
